About Erns Consultant

Healthcare training with a professional, student-centered approach.

Who We Are

Erns Consultant provides training support for students interested in sterile processing and central service technician careers. Our goal is to help students understand core healthcare safety practices and prepare for the next step in their professional journey.

Our Focus

We focus on practical learning, professional conduct, infection control, instrument handling, sterilization, packaging, and review support.
